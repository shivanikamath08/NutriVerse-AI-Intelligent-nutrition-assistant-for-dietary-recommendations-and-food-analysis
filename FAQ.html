Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use NutriVerse AI?

Create your account or sign in, then upload a clear photo of your meal. NutriVerse AI will analyze and provide an instant nutrition breakdown and personalized tips.

How accurate is the food detection?

Our AI model is trained on extensive datasets, achieving good accuracy for common foods. Accuracy depends on image quality and complexity of the meal.

What if my dish is not recognized?

If detection fails, you can manually edit ingredients to ensure accurate nutrition calculation.

How do portion and piece inputs affect nutrition?

For food items like donuts or idlis, inputting the number of pieces allows precise calculations. Otherwise, portion sizes (e.g., small, medium, large) scale the nutritional values.

Where do you get your nutrition data?

We use a comprehensive nutrition database compiled from trusted sources formatted into CSV files, mapping ingredients to their nutritional content.

Is my data safe with NutriVerse?

Your data, including uploaded images, is securely handled and not stored or shared beyond the analysis process.

Can I track my diet with NutriVerse?

NutriVerse helps in understanding nutrition, though for medical advice and detailed tracking, consult professional dietitians.
