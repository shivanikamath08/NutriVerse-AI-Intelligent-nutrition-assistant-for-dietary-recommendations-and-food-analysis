How NutriVerse AI Works

User logs in or creates an account.
User uploads a clear photo of their meal.
Photo is sent to our secure backend server.
Backend uses YOLO AI model to detect the dish and ingredients in the image.
Detected ingredients are matched with nutrition data from our extensive database.
The system calculates total calories, protein, carbs, and fat using standard nutritional formulas.
Analyzed information and personalized health suggestions are sent back to the frontend.
User reviews the nutrition report and can adjust quantities or portion sizes if needed.
Upon adjustment, the system recalculates nutrition values for updated accuracy.
User logs out or proceeds to analyze another meal at their convenience.
